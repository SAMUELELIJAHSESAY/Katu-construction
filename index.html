About Us

Learn about our company, our values, and our commitment to excellence

Your Trusted Construction Partner in Sierra Leone

KA-TU Construction and Engineering Company Ltd is a premier construction firm based in Freetown, Sierra Leone. With years of industry experience, we've built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovation in the construction sector.

We specialize in a wide range of construction services, from residential and commercial buildings to large-scale infrastructure projects. Our team of skilled professionals is committed to delivering projects on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Foday Turay

Founder & CEO
